01

Housing type sets the biggest price gap.

Hotel rooms and entire homes command a clear premium. A private room costs about 43% less than an entire home on average.

02

Central districts cost more.

Mitte leads the market at €146.6 per night, while Reinickendorf is the lowest-priced district at €87.4.

03

Space matters more than reputation.

Capacity has a strong relationship with price (r = 0.59). Ratings are nearly uncorrelated with it (r = 0.03).

04

Correlations point to the next modelling step.

Bedrooms, beds and capacity belong in a price model. Ratings and review count should not be assumed to carry predictive value without further features.
